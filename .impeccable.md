## Design Context

### Users
Primary audience is potential clients, with recruiters as a secondary audience. Visitors are evaluating credibility and fit quickly, usually with limited time and high comparison against other portfolios.

### Brand Personality
The experience should feel technically credible, confident, and premium. Tone should stay concise and professional while still feeling human.

### Aesthetic Direction
Use a mostly monochrome visual system with one accent color for emphasis and interaction highlights. The interface should feel refined and intentional, and explicitly avoid generic "corporate AI template" aesthetics.

### Design Principles
1. Lead with trust: prioritize signals of competence, clarity, and reliability in the first screen.
2. Keep visual restraint: minimal structure, strong typography, and deliberate spacing over decorative complexity.
3. Use accent color with intent: reserve color for hierarchy, calls to action, and key interaction states.
4. Communicate premium confidence: favor precise alignment, consistent rhythm, and calm motion over flashy effects.
5. Maintain accessible defaults: preserve standard good-practice accessibility across contrast, focus visibility, semantic structure, and interaction feedback.
